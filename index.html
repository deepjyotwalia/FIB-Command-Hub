/me takes out bodycam and attaches it to belt, hides it, checks its ballistic and waterproof
/me makes sure the hidden bodycam is recording and checks for the red light
Refreshing & Saving
/me refreshes the bodycam and checks that it is still recording
/me saves the bodycam footage, uploads it to FIB servers, and continues recording
