Bodycam Commands

Uniform & Undercover
Attaching Bodycam (Uniform)
/me takes out the bodycam, attaches it to chest, checks that it is ballistic and waterproof
/me ensures the bodycam is recording and checks for the red light
/do The bodycam is recording, ballistic and waterproof
/me connects the PDA to the nearest SAHP cell tower and cloud servers
Attaching Bodycam (Undercover)
/me takes out the bodycam, clips it to the belt and hides it, checks that it is ballistic and waterproof
/me makes sure the hidden bodycam is recording and checks for the red light
Refreshing & Saving
/me refreshes the bodycam and checks that it is still recording
/me saves the bodycam footage, uploads it to SAHP servers, and continues recording

⚡ Tip: Start your bodycam or screen recording first, then type the command while you are clearly visible on camera.
